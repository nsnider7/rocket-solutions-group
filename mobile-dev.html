Our Process

Gather the Requirements

We make sure that our team understands the complete idea of your app, our requirements team will have a meeting with you first. Then we take a day to analyze and list questions or suggestions using our experience and view the project from all technical aspects.

Solidify the Technical Specifications

All the requirements discussed in the initial meetings are then documented to ensure that we have a written scope of the project which we can use as a reference at any point during development.

Prototyping and UI/UX

We design your app in adobe xd. We will share the updates quite frequently and your feedback will be required constantly as we design the app so we both are on the same page and going in the correct direction.

Development of Screen Design

We develop a real app that has screens only without functionality yet in this stage, in this phase you’ll be able to install the test app on your phone and can assess the quality of UI.

Development of Screen Functionality

In this phase, we will develop the server and database needed for your app.
